## Image Requirements

### Technical Specifications:
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Resolution:** Minimum 800x800px (prefer 1200x1200px)
- **Background:** White or transparent
- **View:** Front view of device
- **Quality:** Official product photography (no mockups or renders)

### Naming Convention:
- **Format:** `{brand}-{model-slug}.jpg`
- **Example:** `apple-iphone-17-pro-max.jpg`
- **Lowercase:** All filenames in lowercase with hyphens
